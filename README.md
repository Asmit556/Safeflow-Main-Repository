# SafeFlow: AI-Based Glacial Lake Outburst Flood (GLOF) Risk Prediction System

## Project Overview

**SafeFlow** is a multi-modal artificial intelligence framework designed to predict and monitor the risk of **Glacial Lake Outburst Floods (GLOFs)** using satellite-based environmental data and deep learning models. The system integrates glacier dynamics, temperature forecasting, and precipitation prediction to estimate the probability of potential flood events.

By combining climate indicators and geospatial data, SafeFlow enables **proactive disaster monitoring**, helping authorities and researchers identify high-risk situations before catastrophic flooding occurs.

The project focuses on vulnerable Himalayan regions such as the **Chorabari Lake basin**, where rapid glacier melt and expanding glacial lakes increase the likelihood of sudden outburst floods.

## Problem Statement

Climate change has accelerated glacier melting across mountain regions, leading to the formation and expansion of glacial lakes. Many of these lakes are contained by unstable moraine dams, which can suddenly fail and release massive volumes of water downstream.

Traditional monitoring methods rely on satellite imagery and manual interpretation, which often detect hazards only after dangerous conditions develop. This project addresses this limitation by developing an **AI-based predictive system** that analyzes environmental patterns to identify potential GLOF risks in advance.

## Project Goals

### Develop a Predictive Risk Model

Build deep learning models capable of forecasting environmental factors that influence glacial lake instability.

### Enable Continuous Environmental Monitoring

Use satellite and climate datasets to track glacier movement, temperature changes, and precipitation trends.

### Generate Early Warning Indicators

Produce a probability-based **GLOF risk score** and trigger alerts when environmental conditions exceed defined thresholds.

---

## Key Features

### Multi-Model Deep Learning Architecture

SafeFlow integrates three specialized models:

* **Terracast (Transformer Model)**
  Predicts glacier surface velocity.

* **Tempcast (Stacked LSTM Model)**
  Forecasts temperature variations affecting glacier melt.

* **Raincast (Hybrid CNN-LSTM Model)**
  Predicts precipitation intensity and rainfall patterns.

---

### Weighted Ensemble Risk Scoring

The predictions from all models are combined using a **weighted ensemble method** to generate a probability-based **GLOF risk score**.

---

### Operational Trigger Thresholds

The system identifies high-risk conditions based on environmental thresholds:

* Glacier surface velocity increase **> 200 m/year**
* Temperature anomaly **> 6°C**
* Heavy precipitation **> 100 mm**

---

### Early Warning Support

SafeFlow provides actionable insights that can support disaster management teams in issuing early warnings and preparing mitigation strategies.

## System Architecture

SafeFlow processes multiple environmental datasets using specialized deep learning models and combines their outputs to generate a unified risk score.

Workflow:

1. Collect satellite and climate datasets
2. Process data using Terracast, Tempcast, and Raincast models
3. Combine predictions using weighted ensemble learning
4. Calculate probability-based GLOF risk score
5. Display insights on the monitoring dashboard
